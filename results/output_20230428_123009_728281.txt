 The four main themes that emerged from the conversation between the researcher and the teacher about the Math Minds Methods of teaching mathematics are: 
1. The importance of breaking down concepts into smaller steps and providing clear instructions: This theme was selected because it is important for teachers to break down concepts into smaller steps and provide clear instructions in order to ensure that students are understanding the material. 
2. The need to assess student understanding in order to identify gaps in knowledge: This theme was selected because it is important for teachers to assess student understanding in order to identify any gaps in knowledge and to ensure that students are understanding the material. 
3. The importance of providing students with opportunities to experience success in order to foster a positive attitude towards Math: This theme was selected because it is important for teachers to provide students with opportunities to experience success in order to foster a positive attitude towards Math. 
4. The need to use experiences from the current year to inform teaching strategies for the next year, as well as providing students with material from lower grades to ensure success: This theme was selected because it is important for teachers to use experiences from the current year to inform teaching strategies for the next year, as well as providing students with material from lower grades to ensure success.
